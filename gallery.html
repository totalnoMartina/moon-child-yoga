My way to unity

My yoga journey began about three years ago in Ireland when I joined yoga classes in Dublin city centre. My yoga instructor inspired me with her way of living and the way yoga helped her to become calm, aware and more connected with her inner self. I have decided to sign up to Marina Yoga and Reiki School in Krabi, Thailand for the TTC 200h Allianz Hatha Yoga and Meditation Course. The entire training lasted one month and there were only 3 students in each class.

Dominika Urbanska
